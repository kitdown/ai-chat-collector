What this extension does

💾

Auto-collect

Every chat you open on Claude, ChatGPT, or Grok is saved automatically. URL, title, timestamp.

🏷

Smart tags

Auto-groups similar chats. "facilitation", "facilitate" become one tag.

🎲

Random chat

One click opens a random old chat. Review it, keep it or clean it up.

🗑

Auto-cleanup

Deleted chats are detected and removed from your collection automatically.

How it works

1

Just use your AI tools as usual

The extension silently saves every chat you open on Claude, ChatGPT, and Grok. No buttons to press, no workflow changes.

2

Click the extension icon

See your chat count by platform, top tags, and the "Random Chat" button. Or click "Manage all chats" for the full view.

3

Explore, filter, clean up

Filter by platform. Use tags to find related chats. Open random ones to review. Delete what you no longer need.
